Soccer Euro Cup 2016 Totomat Rules


How does it work?

You guess the results of the soccer games of the European Soccer Cup 2016 and get points if you were right.


Price

Every participant contributes with 5 Swiss Francs to the pot. After the Euro cup, the first ranked wins the pot minus 5 Swiss Francs. The last ranked gets his entry money returned (i.e. 5 Swiss Francs).


Dates

Latest date to hand in your guessed results for the group phase.

25th of June 2016 15 h

Latest date to hand in your guessed results for the group of 16.

30th of June 2016 21 h

Latest date to hand in your guessed results for quarter finals.

6th of July 2016 21 h

Latest date to hand in your guessed results for the semi finals.

10th of July 2016 21 h

Latest date to hand in your guessed result for the final.

Points

You get one point if you guessed the outcome of the game right (i.e. which country wins or if it is a tie). You get another point if you guessed the difference of goals correct. If you even guessed the result correct, you get another point. If the game is a tie, you automatically predicted the goal-difference correctly. In that case, you cannot get additional points. Thus, the maximum amount of points to win per game is three, except if it is a tie. In that case, it would only be two points. Some examples to clarify:

If several players have the same amount of points, the amount of correct winner guesses determines who is higher ranked.


Additionally, you can give two guesses for the finalists. If your first guess wins the tournament, you get 10 points. If your first guess ends second, you get 5 points. If your second guess wins the tournament or becomes second, you get 5 points.
